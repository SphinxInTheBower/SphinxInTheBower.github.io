About the Authors

Sophia Taborski is a third-year Ph.D. student in the Classics Department at Cornell University, with a focus on ancient art. Sophia branched out to twentieth century Canandaigua and Mary Clark Thompson for this online book.

Blake Taylor is a first-year M.A. student with the Cornell Institute of Archaeology and Material Studies (CIAMS), with an interest in conservation, preservation, and presentation, especially of textiles. Blake created the graphics and media for this online book.

Amanda House is a final-semester undergraduate student in Computer Science (major) and Art History (minor) at Cornell University. Amanda wrote the code for this website and is the ongoing webmaster.
